Poem

          Dream Menu

          Let’s take a trip that’s unlike any other
          It’s gonna be pretty yummy!
          Your tastebuds will tour the cuisines I’ve discovered
          From some of my favourite countries.

          So sit at this table that’s packed with lush foods
          I’ve prepared them especially for you.
          There’s some knives and some forks, and some chopsticks to use
          (Don’t worry – I’m crap with them too!)

          Our first stop’s the Greek island of Skiathos
          For a dish with a tasty combo.
          Fresh feta and mint that’s been crumbled and tossed
          And then baked into parcels of filo.

          Zap!  Zing!  The cheese is so melty!
          The mint makes my tongue want to dance!
          I could eat these forever, but that wouldn’t help me…
          …my waistline wouldn’t stand a chance!

          Next on our trip, we’ll be crossing the seas
          To the beautiful land of Japan.
          A donburi bowl that’s piled high with goodies
          Can you finish it?  I know I can!

          Fluffy white rice, and tofu that’s chopped
          With seaweed and teriyaki glaze
          Did you see they put mayonnaise over the top?
          It goes pretty well – I’m amazed!

          Do you fancy a side dish for us to devour?
          Let’s add Ireland onto our trip.
          Their mashed potatoes are quite different from ours
          And possibly better, I’d quip!

          You’ll find no lumps here – they’re creamy and smooth
          And laced with hot butter and cabbage.
          A veggie that most people will not approve
          But in this case, they’ll be truly ravaged!

          Now it’s time for dessert, and it’s gonna be good
          It’s the best pudding England can muster.
          There’s no better dish to sum up my childhood
          Than my Mum’s apple crumble with custard.

          The cinnamon warmth, and the sharpness of fruit
          It’s like a big hug in my belly.
          Its comforting vibes were too much to compute
          I’d fall asleep in front of the telly!

          After such a delightful, delectable meal
          We all deserve a nice drink.
          Which country has one that’s the most ideal?
          America’s got it, I think.

          A milkshake with chocolate of the darkest class
          And hazelnuts blended within
          Liquid Ferrero Roche in a glass?
          That’s definitely bound to win!

          Oh man, I’m stuffed!  This meal has been great!
          Many a morsel we’ve tried.
          I like to travel the world through the food on my plate
          And I hope you enjoyed the ride!
